About Us

Welcome to Tiger Trail Adventure & Tour, one of the oldest and most trusted tour agencies in Sauraha, Chitwan. Founded by a passionate local nature guide, our journey began with the vision of sharing the unparalleled beauty and adventure of Chitwan with visitors from around the globe.

At Tiger Trail, we specialize in creating unforgettable experiences tailored to your preferences. Whether you're seeking thrilling jungle activities, convenient air or bus ticketing, comfortable hotel reservations, cozy homestays, or serene religious tours, we’ve got you covered—all at reasonable prices.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service with a deep understanding of the region's natural and cultural treasures. With years of expertise and a commitment to excellence, we ensure every moment of your trip is safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

Let us take you on an adventure where nature meets hospitality. Discover Chitwan like never before with Tiger Trail Adventure & Tour—your gateway to the heart of Nepal.
